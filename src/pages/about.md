I am interested in making spaces, systems, and stories feel more human.

This site is a personal archive in public. It holds a short professional profile, a timeline of experience, a running journal, and a small set of photographs. I wanted one place that felt slower than social media and more personal than a resume.

## What I care about

- Clear writing
- Thoughtful product and technical work
- Visual atmosphere, especially in quiet or transitional spaces
- Building a body of work that feels coherent over time

## Working style

I like projects that mix structure and interpretation. That usually means work where research, implementation, and expression are all part of the same process.
